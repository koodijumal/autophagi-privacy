Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

Autophagi ("the App") is a free fasting companion app. Your privacy matters — this policy explains what data we collect and why.

1. Data We Collect

Locally stored data only. The App stores the following on your device using local storage (Hive):

  • Fasting start time
  • Elapsed time and progress
  • Dismissed guidance card IDs

This data never leaves your device. We do not have servers, accounts, or cloud sync. We cannot see, access, or retrieve any of your data.

2. Advertising

The App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

3. Affiliate Links

Some guidance cards contain optional product links (e.g., Amazon). Tapping these opens your browser — we may earn a small commission on purchases. These links do not collect data within the App.

4. External Links

The App contains a link to an external tip jar service (Buy Me a Coffee). This third-party site has its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for the practices of external sites.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with a new "Effective date" at the top of this page.
